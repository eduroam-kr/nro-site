The eduroam Terms of Use have been revised as follows and will take effect from March 1, 2025.

## Key Revisions

- **Article 4 (User Obligations)**: Clarified prohibition on sharing institutional account credentials
- **Article 6 (Service Restrictions)**: Added criteria for temporary restrictions in cases of excessive bandwidth usage
- **Article 8 (Personal Data Processing)**: Adjusted connection log retention period (90 days → 180 days)

## Effective Date

Effective from **March 1, 2025**.

The revised terms are available on the [Policy page](/en/policy/).
