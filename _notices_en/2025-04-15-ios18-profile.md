date: 2025-04-15

Following the iOS 18 update, some devices have been found unable to connect to eduroam.

## Symptoms

- eduroam authentication fails after updating to iOS 18
- Previously saved Wi-Fi profiles become deactivated

## Steps to Fix

1. Go to **Settings > Wi-Fi**, tap **(i)** next to eduroam → **Forget This Network**
2. Go to **Settings > General > VPN & Device Management** and remove the existing eduroam profile
3. Reinstall the latest profile from [cat.eduroam.org](https://cat.eduroam.org)
4. Reconnect to eduroam and verify authentication

## Inquiries

If the issue persists after following the steps above, please contact the helpdesk.  
Email: [helpdesk@sandbox.re.kr](mailto:helpdesk@sandbox.re.kr)
