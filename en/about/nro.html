National Roaming Operator

The NRO is the official national operator of eduroam

The NRO is designated by GÉANT, the pan-European research and education network, to represent and operate eduroam services within a specific country.

Key Responsibilities of an NRO

National Infrastructure

Operates the national RADIUS servers to relay authentication requests between institutions.

Institution Management

Reviews membership applications and ensures policy compliance across all member institutions.

Global Connectivity

Connects to GÉANT and partner NROs worldwide to enable international roaming.

Policy & Outreach

Establishes national eduroam policies and promotes service adoption.

NRO in Korea

In Korea, KREN (Korea Research and Education Network) serves as the designated eduroam NRO, overseeing the membership and operations of domestic universities and research institutions. Technical infrastructure is jointly operated with KISTI (Korea Institute of Science and Technology Information).

KISTI (Technical Operations)
  • National RADIUS server deployment and operation
  • Global eduroam network interconnection
  • Technical support and incident response
KREN (NRO)
  • Designated national eduroam operator
  • Institution membership review and policy setting
  • International liaison with global eduroam organizations
  • Service promotion and outreach
