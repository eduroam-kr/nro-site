eduroam Operations Center

The domestic eduroam service is operated through a collaborative partnership between the Korea Research Environment Open Network (KREONET / KISTI) and Korea Education and Research Information Service (KERIS / KREN).

Planning & Management (KREN)
  • Membership approval and institution management
  • Service policy and promotion
Technical Operations (KISTI)
  • National RADIUS server operations
  • Global eduroam interconnection
