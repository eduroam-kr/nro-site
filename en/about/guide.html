eduroam Membership Guide

eduroam membership is applied for directly by the server administrator of a university or research institution.
Please follow the 4-step process below in order.

STEP 01
Register on eduroam.kr

The server administrator of the university or institution must register directly on the eduroam.kr website. Only network administrators may apply — general faculty and students do not need to register separately.

  • Applicant: network server administrator of the university or institution
  • General faculty and students do not need to register
  • Enter username, password, email, and institution information

STEP 02
Administrator Approval

The Operations Center reviews the application and grants university server administrator privileges. Once approved, a notification email is sent to the registered address, and access to the management system becomes available.

  • Operations Center staff reviews the application
  • Notification email sent upon approval
  • Access to server administrator menu granted after approval

STEP 03
Register Institution & Auth Server

After approval, log in to the eduroam.kr management system and enter your institution name, address, application form, and RADIUS authentication server details. Technical coordination with the Operations Center follows.

  • Register institution name, address, and contact information
  • Configure RADIUS server IP and port
  • Technical coordination with Operations Center for server integration
  • Upload signed membership application form

STEP 04
Register Campus Location

Register your campus location and contact details for display on the global eduroam map. Once registered, your institution will appear on the worldwide eduroam map.

  • Enter campus GPS coordinates (latitude & longitude)
  • Register contact name, email, and phone number
  • Institution displayed on the global eduroam map
  • Service operation begins after registration is complete
