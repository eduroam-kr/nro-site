What is eduroam?

eduroam is a global Wi-Fi roaming service for the
research and education community. With a single setup,
you can connect automatically at institutions in 101+ countries.

Learn more

What is NRO?

NRO (National Roaming Operator) is the organization
responsible for operating eduroam in each country.
In Korea, KREN serves as the designated NRO.
